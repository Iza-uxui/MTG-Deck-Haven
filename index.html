Welcome

Whether you’re new to Magic: The Gathering (MTG) or a seasoned spell-slinger refining your next masterpiece, you’ve come to the right place. Here, we focus on what makes Magic truly exciting — building decks that reflect your playstyle, creativity, and strategy.

Explore guides on formats, archetypes, and themes to help you craft decks that are both powerful and personal. Learn how to balance creatures and spells, fine-tune your mana base, and find synergy between your favorite cards.

From casual kitchen-table play to competitive events, our goal is to help you understand why decks work — not just what to play. Dive in, experiment, and discover the joy of bringing your ideas to life on the battlefield.

Welcome to the heart of deckbuilding. Let’s get shuffling.

Resources

Official Databases and Tools

  • Scryfall: Comprehensive card search with filters for keywords, mechanics, legality, and flavor.
  • Gatherer: Wizards of the Coast’s official card database.
  • MTG Arena: Magic Online deck builders — Built-in tools for creating and testing decks digitally.

Deck-Building and Sharing Sites

  • Moxfield: Popular for building, organizing, and sharing decks (especially for Commander).
  • Archidekt: Visual deckbuilder with drag-and-drop functionality and price tracking.
  • TappedOut: Long-running community site for posting decks and getting feedback.
  • MTGGoldfish: Offers decklists, metagame analysis, and budget-friendly builds.

Content and Community Inspiration

  • YouTube channels like The Command Zone, Tolarian Community College, and MTGGoldfish showcase strategies, gameplay, and deck techs.
  • Podcasts and Twitch streams discuss deckbuilding trends and Commander brews.
  • Reddit (r/magicTCG, r/EDH, r/BudgetBrews) offer discussion, feedback, and inspiration.
  • Discord communities and local game stores allow players to share ideas and test decks together.
